Swedish Meatball Recipe

Ingredients (4-6 servings)

  1. 500 g (18 oz) ground (minced) beef/pork mixture
  2. 250 ml (1.25 cup) milk
  3. 75 g (0.75 cup) white breadcrumbs
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1 onion
  6. salt, white pepper
  7. ground allspice

Instructions

Chop all the ingredients and add them into a bowl while stirring. Roll it into balls and put it in the pan.
