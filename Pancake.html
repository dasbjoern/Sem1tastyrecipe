Swedish Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup oat flour, loosely packed (50g)
  2. 1/4 tsp salt
  3. pinch uncut stevia OR 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
  4. 1/2 cup water (minus 1 tbsp if using maple)
  5. 2 tbsp applesauce or mashed banana

Instructions

Add all ingredients into a bowl and stirr. Pour it into a pan and fry it on both sides.
